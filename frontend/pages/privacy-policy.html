Privacy Policy

How Nucleomic processes files, cookies, and personal data while providing sequence analysis services.

Last updated: 2026

1. Introduction

Nucleomic ("we", "our", "us") operates the website located at https://nucleomic.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in a transparent and secure way.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can exercise your rights.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Log and usage data

We may collect technical data such as:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type, device information, and operating system
  • Pages visited, timestamps, and basic interaction data

2.2 Uploaded files

When you upload sequence files (e.g., AB1 or FASTA files) to use our tools, these files are processed solely for analysis purposes. We do not sell or share your uploaded files with third parties.

2.3 Cookies and similar technologies

We may use cookies for:

  • Session and security management
  • Basic analytics and performance
  • Advertising, through Google AdSense

3. Google AdSense

Our website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to show personalised or non-personalised ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

You can manage or opt out of personalised advertising via Google's Ads Settings page.

4. How We Use the Information

  • To operate and improve Nucleomic's analysis tools and services
  • To maintain security and prevent misuse or abuse of the platform
  • To monitor performance, troubleshoot issues, and improve user experience
  • To display relevant advertisements where applicable

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share limited information with:

  • Google (for AdSense and advertising)
  • Analytics or error-tracking providers (if enabled)
  • Public authorities when required to comply with applicable law

6. Data Retention

Uploaded files are stored only as long as required to provide the analysis and maintain service functionality. Log and technical data may be retained for a reasonable period for security and analytics.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. You may also object to certain types of processing.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: contact@nucleomic.com.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
